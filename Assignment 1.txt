Answer-1
The -p flag tells the mkdir command to create the main directory first if it doesn’t already exist . The words in the brackets are part of the “brace expansion list”. Each of the items in the brace expansion list is appended separately to the preceding path .

mkdir -p a/{b,c} b/{d,e} c/{f,g} d/{i,j} g/{k,l,z} i/{m/z}
